WCIF (abbreviation): "Where Can I Find"
A commonly used acronym in online communication to inquire about the location or availability of specific information, resources, products, or services.
In this case, "WCIF" is refered to where to find the FFXIV© texture modification packages used to make "the Black Cat" a reality.
The futa enjoyer asked me: "WCIF the horsecock "the Black Cat" has?"
I had way too much fun writing this lmao
Remember to follow the Original Character Terms of Use.
